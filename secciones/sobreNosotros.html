Who are we here at Critical Hit?

Critical Hit was born as a solution to a problem that we felt the ttrpg community has had for years.
All too often we saw our own campaigns and gamenights limited by the lack of community and a dedicated space.
We felt that other hobbies had homes and centres of community engagment where people could gather and enjoy with
likeminded people, but not tabletop rpg's. So we decided, hey, what's stopping us from creating our own dream space?.

So we did...and people seemed to enjoy this growing community.
You enjoyed it so much that our services grew, we were able to begin catering not only to the ttrpg crowd, but the board game wants of our community as well.
Through the years we have been able to grow the catalogue of manuals and resources we offer,
But we also grew our physical space which meant we could offer a space where you could come play and stay, enjoy a coffee with friends and freely access all of our in-house games available with the rental of a table.
We love what we do and we love the community that grew around our home, so we invite you to come, hang out and see for yourself!.

Steve, Joe, Lee and Elliott.
